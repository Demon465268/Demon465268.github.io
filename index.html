Who are we?

ADAGames is an independent indie game company, created by young developers from St. Petersburg, Russia. The company's slogan: "We do what we want". Now we are working on our first project - a Christmas side-scroller game, but this is just the beginning. We do not plan to stop at this game and in parallel we are engaged in several more projects that will soon see the light.
